Captivate Interiors vision is to use sustainable and eco friendly products with our projects, we mainly use locally sourced material to make our products and to support local businesses throughout New Zealand. Although it might be challenging for some situations, our talented and amazing designers always find a way to make things work and produce a stunning products and designs.

Our recent projects


Restaurant

This wooden themed restaurant is based from the owner's personal taste and our company's vision.

Choosing eco-friendly and sustainable materials which were sourced locally is one of the strong suits of the restaurant, the tables and chairs were designed by one of our own intertior designer.

The rustic materials such as the bar top and slabs made this design even more suitable for the theme of the restaurant.



Hotel

This amazing hotel located in the heart of Tauranga was designed with a sustainable materials touched up with a stunning wall plant which is undoubtedly an eye catcher for visitors and hotel guests.

Easily sustainable plants are placed from the hotel lobby to the hotel rooms to give a nature friendly environment. Crafted with our very own designs gives this hotel a stunning mixture of modern and rustic look and atmosphere.

We used various type of metals, tiles and glasses in the hotel lobby, and the result is atmospheric and luxurious, a real experience.



Office

Modern, fresh and functional was the main concept for this stunning office located in Auckland, CBD.

The open area idea is to encourage collaborative work within the organisation. We included plants all over the office to bring outside in, and to take out the idea that this office is within the busiest area in Auckland.

The stairs and staff areas are painted with white to match the wall and railings, giving the office a neutral atmosphere, the paint also compliments the plant by giving it more contrast.


Home

This beautiful home located at Takapuna was a collaborative design with the owner and our designers.

We mixed and matched different materials such as the wooden floor and wooden cabinets to give a simple yet classic outcome.

Wooden materials were place in the kitchen, living room and bedrooms to keep the concept consistent. This design is the exact representation of cozy and modern home.
